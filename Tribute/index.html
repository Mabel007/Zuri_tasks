Onika Tanya Maraj-Petty

Is a Trinidadian-born[a] rapper, singer, and songwriter based in the United States. She is often regarded as one of the greatest female rappers of her generation, as well as being one of the most influential rappers of all time.

Here is a timeline of Onika Maraj-petty's life :

  • 1982 -Born on December 8, in the Saint James district of Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago.
  • 1987 - Minaj's father was addicted to alcohol and crack cocaine, and had a violent temper,he burned down their house.
  • 2001 -Minaj graduated, and wanted to become an actress, and she was cast in the Off-Broadway play In Case You Forget in 2001.
  • 2004 - She was briefly signed with Brooklyn group Full Force, in which she rapped in a quartet called The Hoodstars composed of Lou$tar (son of "Bowlegged Lou"), Safaree Samuels (Scaff Beezy) and 7even Up.
  • 2007 - Fendi, CEO of Brooklyn label Dirty Money Entertainment, signed Minaj to his label in 2007 under a 180-day contract.
  • 2008 - 2009 - Minaj released her first mixtape, Playtime Is Over, on July 5, 2007,and her second, Sucka Free, on April 12, 2008.She released her third mixtape, Beam Me Up Scotty, on April 18, 2009
  • 2010 - 2011 - Minaj made her first two appearances on the U.S Billboard Hot 100 chart with her features on "Knockout" and "Up Out My Face" by Lil Wayne and Mariah Carey respectively
  • 2012 - 2013 - "Starships" was released in February 2012 as the lead single from Minaj's forthcoming second album Pink Friday: Roman Reloaded.[78] The song reached number five on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100,[33] and went on to become the fifth best-selling single of 2012.[
  • 2014 - 2017 - Minaj's first live-action theatrical film The Other Woman was filmed in spring 2013 and premiered on April 25, 2014.[107] In late 2014, Minaj separated from her longtime boyfriend Safaree Samuels, whom she had dated since 2003
  • 2018 - 2019 - She released the Queen album and got married
  • 2020 - 2021 - She gave birth to a son on September 30, 2020, and released beam me up scotty mixtape In May 2021
